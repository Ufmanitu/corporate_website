Common Questions

Before you reach out.

What size of engagement do you take on?▾
We work with organizations of all sizes, from high-growth scale-ups to global enterprises. Our smallest engagements typically start at €150K and our largest run into eight figures. What matters more than size is fit: we take on work where we believe we can make a real difference, and we turn down work where we don't believe that — even when it's lucrative.
How quickly can you mobilize a team?▾
We can typically mobilize a full team within two weeks of contract signature. For urgent situations — a crisis, an imminent decision, or a time-sensitive opportunity — we've moved meaningfully faster than that. Speed of mobilization is something we've deliberately built into how we operate, because many of the problems worth solving can't wait six weeks for a kickoff meeting.
Do you work with clients outside your listed industries?▾
Yes, selectively. While we have particularly deep expertise in our six focus sectors, we take on engagements in adjacent areas where our core capabilities — rigorous analysis, strategic clarity, technology advisory, and operational redesign — clearly translate. If you're unsure whether we're the right fit, tell us what you're working on and we'll give you an honest answer.
Will the partner I meet be the partner who leads the engagement?▾
Yes. We don't pitch with senior partners and then hand work to junior teams. The partner you meet during the sales process is the same person who leads your engagement, attends your steering committee meetings, and stays accountable for outcomes throughout. This is a point of real differentiation for us — and one we actively protect as we grow.
How do you handle confidentiality?▾
We sign NDAs before any substantive conversation, and we treat client confidentiality with the same seriousness as our own reputation. We never disclose client identities or project details without explicit permission — which is why our case studies refer to industries and outcomes rather than names wherever clients prefer it. The relationships we value most are built on absolute trust, and we act accordingly.

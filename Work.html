Apex
Core Banking Infrastructure Overhaul

From 30-year-old mainframe to cloud-native microservices — in 18 months.

Apex Financial's core banking system was a constraint on every strategic initiative the leadership team wanted to pursue. Built in the 1990s, it was reliable but inflexible — unable to support real-time processing, open banking APIs, or the personalization capabilities their customers increasingly expected from challengers.

Meridian led the full transformation: architectural blueprint, vendor selection, phased migration, and organizational change management. We delivered a zero-downtime cutover on a system processing over £2.4B in daily transactions.
